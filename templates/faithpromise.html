What is Faith Promise Missions?

Faith Promise Missions is a way of giving to world evangelism.
Rather than waiting to respond to individual appeals as they come along,
believers ask the Lord how much they should give to missions over the
next year. They then committ to that annual total as a "Faith Promise."

"They gave of themselves as much as they were able,
and even beyond their ability. See that you also excel in this
grace of giving...Your plenty will supply what they need."

*Faith Promise giving above and beyond.
(II Corinthians 8:2-3 II Corinthians 9:6-15)

*Faith Promise giving reflects our personal commitments.
(II Corinthians 8:5)

*Though Faith Promise giving is optional,
it does clearly demonstrate our love.
(Philippians 2:1-4; Luke 6:38, Romans 12:9-10)
